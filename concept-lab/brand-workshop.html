A practical decision

Pick one primary system; keep weather colors for data, not decoration.

  1. Choose the mark that feels distinctive at 32px and at a large wordmark size.
  2. Use its ink color for navigation and typography, its accent sparingly for actions, and reserve radar/map colors for weather meaning.
  3. Then test the chosen direction on the existing Dashboard, Forecast, Class, and HQ shells before replacing the live mark.
